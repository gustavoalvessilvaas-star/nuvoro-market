# Manual Operations

## Fulfillment Flow

1. Customer buys on Nuvoro Market.
2. Order appears in admin.
3. Store owner purchases from supplier manually.
4. Store owner enters supplier order ID.
5. Store owner enters tracking code when available.
6. Customer receives tracking email.
7. Fulfillment status is updated in admin.

## Product Review Rules

Only add products with clear utility, clean claims and verified supplier terms. Keep copy benefit-focused and avoid absolute claims such as guaranteed safety, cures, prevents all problems or unrealistic delivery promises.

Avoid these categories at launch unless compliance, supplier reliability and advertising policy risk have been reviewed:

- Supplements
- Health products with strong claims
- Cosmetics with sensitive claims
- Skin tag removers
- Replicas or trademarked products
- Weapons or sharp items
- Expensive electronics
- Drones
- Smartwatches from unknown suppliers
- Products with batteries unless supplier and shipping compliance are verified
- Clothing with complex sizing

Before adding a supplier-backed product, verify product quality, shipping time to the United States, tracking reliability, battery or shipping restrictions, supplier reviews, refund conditions, return conditions and product compliance.
