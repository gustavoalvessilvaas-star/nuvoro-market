# Product Validation Framework

Score every candidate from 1 to 5.

- Demand score
- Wow factor score
- Margin score
- Logistics risk
- Compliance risk
- Supplier confidence
- Creative potential

Recommended statuses:

- idea
- researching
- sample ordered
- approved
- rejected
- testing
- winner

Avoid supplements, strong health claims, trademarked/replica products, weapons, expensive electronics, unknown battery products and products with complex sizing until compliance and logistics are verified.
