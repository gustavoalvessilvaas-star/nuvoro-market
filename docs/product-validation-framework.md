# Product Validation Framework

Use `/admin/product-validation` before adding products publicly.

## Scores

Score each field from 1 to 10:

- demand score
- wow factor score
- margin score
- logistics risk score
- compliance risk score
- supplier confidence score
- creative potential score

The admin board totals the scores.

## Recommendation

- 80-100: strong candidate
- 60-79: test carefully
- 40-59: needs more research
- below 40: reject

## Statuses

- `idea`
- `researching`
- `sample_ordered`
- `sample_received`
- `approved`
- `rejected`
- `testing`
- `winner`
- `paused`

## Convert To Product Draft

Use `Convert to Product Draft` only after supplier, cost, shipping and compliance notes are reviewed. The created product is saved as `draft`, not public.

Avoid supplements, strong health claims, trademarked or replica products, weapons, expensive electronics, unknown battery products and products with complex sizing until compliance and logistics are verified.
