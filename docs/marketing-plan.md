# Marketing Plan

## Brand Positioning

- Brand: Nuvoro Market
- Slogan: Smart finds for everyday living.
- Niche: Smart Everyday Essentials

## Target Audience

United States consumers aged 25-44, including online shoppers looking for practical solutions, homeowners, apartment renters, pet owners, drivers, remote workers and practical gadget buyers.

## Initial Content Strategy

- TikTok
- Instagram Reels
- Pinterest

## First 14-Day Content Plan

1. Problem-focused video for PawTrim LED Grinder.
2. Product demonstration.
3. Before/after grooming angle.
4. UGC-style review.
5. Comparison: traditional nail clipper vs LED grinder.
6. Quick FAQ.
7. Free shipping offer.
8. PowerScrub Drill Brush product introduction.
9. Before/after cleaning video.
10. FreshSeal Vacuum Sealer introduction.
11. Food waste reduction and kitchen organization angle.
12. Top 3 smart finds for your home.
13. Top 3 products pet owners need.
14. Compilation of questions/comments.

## Paid Ads Plan

Start with Meta Ads using Sales objective, United States, age 25-54, broad audience. Test three creatives per product. Start with PawTrim LED Grinder, then PowerScrub Drill Brush Kit, then FreshSeal Compact Vacuum Sealer.

## Metrics

- CTR
- CPC
- AddToCart
- InitiateCheckout
- Purchase
- CPA
- ROAS

## Diagnostics

- Low CTR means weak creative or hook.
- Clicks but no AddToCart means weak page, product or offer.
- AddToCart but no checkout means trust, price or shipping issue.
- Checkout but no purchase means payment friction or total cost issue.
