# Pre-Launch Checklist

1. Domain connected.
2. Support email working.
3. Product images replaced.
4. Supplier verified.
5. Product sample tested.
6. Stripe test purchase completed.
7. Stripe webhook tested.
8. Order created in Supabase.
9. Confirmation email sent.
10. Tracking page tested.
11. Contact form tested.
12. Policies reviewed by a qualified professional.
13. GA4 installed.
14. Meta Pixel installed.
15. Mobile checkout tested.
16. No placeholder images on public pages.
17. No fake reviews.
18. No unrealistic shipping promises.
19. Admin can update order status.
20. Admin can add tracking code.
21. Purchase event recorded.
22. UTM captured on order.

Nuvoro Market is not ready for paid ads until product media, supplier sample testing, Stripe webhook testing and policy review are complete.
