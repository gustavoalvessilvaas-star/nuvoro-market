# Suppliers

Recommended platforms to research manually:

- Zendrop
- CJdropshipping
- DSers + AliExpress
- AutoDS

Before listing a product, verify:

- Product quality
- Shipping time to the United States
- Tracking reliability
- Battery and shipping restrictions
- Supplier reviews
- Refund and return conditions
- Product compliance

Avoid risky launch categories:

- Supplements
- Health products with strong claims
- Cosmetics with sensitive claims
- Skin tag removers
- Replicas or trademarked products
- Weapons or sharp items
- Expensive electronics
- Drones
- Smartwatches from unknown suppliers
- Products with batteries unless supplier and shipping compliance are verified
- Clothing with complex sizing
